MARKET POSITIONING AND AUDIENCE

Samiranna targets couples planning their wedding and individuals looking to capture important life moments. They position themselves as a friendly, approachable, and professional local service, ideal for those who value a personal touch in photography.

FUTURE GOALS

The company aims to grow its presence in the local market, building its portfolio and reputation. They plan to expand their services to include more diverse events, always focusing on capturing the unique essence of each moment.

BRAND IDENTITY AND MARKETING

Samiranna's brand identity is centered around its personal, heartfelt approach to photography. Their marketing focuses on showcasing their portfolio, client testimonials, and the stories behind the moments they capture, appealing to those who seek a photographer with a personal touch and a deep understanding of the significance of life's milestones.
